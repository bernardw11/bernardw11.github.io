Spaghetti and Meatballs


Description

Spaghetti with meatballs is an American dish consisting of spaghetti, tomato sauce and meatballs.
Watch this informative video

    Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 1/4 pound ground sirloin
  • 1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 10 leaves basil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup beef stock

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Place a large pot of water on to boil for spaghetti. When it boils, add salt and pasta.
  3. Roll meat into 1 1/2 inch medium-sized meatballs and place on nonstick cookie sheet or a cookie sheet greased with extra-virgin olive oil.
  4. Heat a deep skillet or medium pot over moderate heat. Add oil, crushed pepper, garlic.
  5. Add beef stock, crushed tomatoes, and herbs. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes.
  6. Toss hot, drained pasta with a few ladles of the sauce and grated cheese
  7. Turn meatballs in remaining sauce. Place pasta on dinner plates and top with meatballs and sauce and extra grated cheese
